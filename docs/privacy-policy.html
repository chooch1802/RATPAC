Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ratpac ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the Ratpac Wagering App ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Phone number (used for account creation and sign-in via SMS verification)
  • Username/handle
  • Account privacy setting (public or private)
  • Wager details you create (activity, amount, terms, opponent)
  • Payment method preference (Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, or Other) and associated handle

Information collected automatically:

  • App usage data (wagers created, subscription status)
  • Device information (operating system, app version)

Information from third parties:

  • Subscription status from Apple App Store (via RevenueCat)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable you to create, accept, and track wagers
  • Process subscription purchases through Apple's in-app purchase system
  • Send SMS verification codes via Twilio
  • Display your public profile and wager history to other users (if your account is public)
  • Track referral codes for influencer campaigns
  • Improve and maintain the App

3. Payments

Ratpac does not process, hold, or transfer money between users. All payments are settled directly between users via third-party peer-to-peer payment services (Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, or other). Ratpac only stores payment method preferences and handles to facilitate this process. We are not responsible for any disputes arising from peer-to-peer payments.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider stores your account data securely
  • Twilio — used to send SMS verification codes to your phone number
  • RevenueCat — used to manage and verify in-app subscription purchases
  • Apple — subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
  • Other users — your handle, win/loss record, and public wagers are visible to other users if your account is set to public
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Age Requirement

You must be 18 years of age or older to use Ratpac. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will terminate their account immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data uses

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database storage via Supabase. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
